# Tucker Carlson — evidence dossier
Updated: 2026-07-18 · Researcher: new-3

By mid-2026 Carlson is the loudest voice of the post-MAGA populist right: culturally hard-right (anti-trans, anti-immigration, Christian-revival framing), economically nationalist and anti-corporate rather than free-market, and radically anti-institution — media, intelligence agencies, expert class, and now Trump himself, whom he broke with over the 2026 Iran War, declaring the MAGA movement has "no future." His placement is far right on the social axis, only mildly right (populist) on economics, and deep in insurgent territory on the system axis.

## Per-question evidence
### e01 · score +2
"A country that doesn't make anything quickly dies." — X, Mar 2025 (https://x.com/TuckerCarlson/status/1902468999511994461)
Repeatedly platformed Lighthizer and Bessent to sell Trump's tariff program approvingly; sustained economic-nationalist advocacy.

### e02 · score 0
No findable commitment either way: he attacks the healthcare industry and libertarian economics but has never endorsed (or campaigned against) a federal insurance guarantee.

### e03 · score 0
He rails at "market worship" and elite wealth but has not endorsed a wealth tax; no concrete statement found for or against.

### e04 · score −1
"Of course, this is not the capitalism I signed up for." — Daily Caller, Sep 2018 (https://dailycaller.com/2018/09/11/not-the-capitalism-i-signed-up-for/)
His signature monologues reject market fundamentalism and treat wage outcomes as a legitimate political concern, cutting against pure market wage-setting; hedged because he backs no specific wage law.

### e05 · score +2
Carlson argued "Google should be regulated like the public utility" it is — Tucker Carlson Tonight, via The Hill, 2017 (https://thehill.com/blogs/pundits-blog/technology/346708-no-tucker-google-shouldnt-be-regulated-as-a-public-utility/)
Repeated, years-long advocacy for antitrust/utility treatment of Big Tech monopolies.

### e06 · score 0
No direct record on Social Security private accounts; his anti-libertarian populism suggests opposition but nothing citable found.

### e07 · score +1
Orbán "decided to affirmatively help Hungarian families grow" rather than abandon them to "hard-edge libertarianism" — Fox broadcasts from Budapest, 2021 (https://www.salon.com/2022/01/28/tucker-carlsons-hungarian-rhapsody-right-wing-nationalism-can-win-the-demographic-war/)
Repeatedly holds up Hungary's state family-subsidy program as a model for the US; rhetorical support, no specific federal bill endorsed.

### e08 · score 0
Hosts crypto-friendly guests but has no findable position on crypto regulation itself.

### e09 · score +1
Platformed Teamsters president Sean O'Brien to discuss "urgent priorities for American workers" — The Tucker Carlson Show, Dec 2024 (https://tuckercarlson.com/tucker-show-sean-obrien)
His populist turn treats organized labor sympathetically; hedged (+1 not +2) given decades of earlier anti-union conservatism.

### e10 · score 0
He attacks universities relentlessly but blames colleges and lenders as villains as much as borrowers; no clean statement on cancellation-as-moral-hazard found.

### e11 · score −2
"The real threat to people is global cooling, otherwise known as winter." — cited in Grist review of his climate record (https://grist.org/culture/how-bad-was-tucker-carlson-for-the-planet/)

"This is not the capitalism I signed up for." — Daily Caller, 2018 (https://dailycaller.com/2018/09/11/not-the-capitalism-i-signed-up-for/)
His Ship of Fools-era populism mocks GOP donor-class orthodoxy that corporate tax relief trickles down to workers; stated opposition, not acted-on policy.

## Social
### s01 · score −2
"Abortion is murder, obviously... it's the same ritual... human sacrifice." — Turning Point USA event, 2023 (https://www.dailysignal.com/2023/09/29/tucker-carlson-declares-abortion-spiritual-battle/)
Calls abortion the one non-negotiable issue; repeated and emphatic.

### s02 · score +2
Mocked the idea "men are child-bearers" as absurdity of trans-inclusive language — Newsweek, 2021 (https://www.newsweek.com/tucker-carlson-mocking-transgender-abortion-advocates-says-men-have-babies-too-1625680)
Long, emphatic record (GLAAD-catalogued) insisting sex is binary in law and language.

### s03 · score +2
Years of emphatic on-air defense of gun rights and attacks on every post-shooting gun-control push (record summarized at https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tucker_Carlson). Treats gun ownership as a core liberty under deliberate assault.

### s04 · score −2
Carlson led nightly attacks on the defund/reimagine-policing movement as civilizational suicide, 2020–2023 (https://www.justsecurity.org/77299/the-tucker-carlson-disinformation-show/). Repeated, emphatic opposition to redirecting police funds.

### s05 · score +2
Named "massive deportation" the top priority for 2025 — TCN interview clip, Jan 2025 (https://www.youtube.com/shorts/zLy497aJV1M)
Combined with his replacement rhetoric, this is repeated, emphatic agreement.

### s06 · score −2
"Americans are being replaced. That is not a conspiracy theory, it is a fact." — Jan 2024, via PolitiFact (https://www.politifact.com/factchecks/2024/jan/25/tucker-carlson/tucker-carlson-is-wrong-when-comparing-migrants-il/)
Demographic-replacement framing treats current immigration levels, legal included, as national dissolution; toured Hungary praising "extremely strict" immigration policy.

### s07 · score −2
The great replacement "is their electoral strategy" — Fox News opinion, 2022 (https://www.foxnews.com/opinion/tucker-carlson-great-replacement-electoral-strategy)
Built a years-long crusade against CRT/systemic-racism curricula as anti-white indoctrination; emphatic disagreement with the statement.

### s08 · score +2
"This is not a political debate, this is a spiritual battle." — 2023, Daily Signal (https://www.dailysignal.com/2023/09/29/tucker-carlson-declares-abortion-spiritual-battle/)
Since 2023 frames US politics as spiritual warfare against Christians and says America is in "a religious revival" (https://www.theblaze.com/news/tucker-carlson-spiritual-battle-christians); wants faith at the center of public life.

### s09 · score +2
Listed "transgender surgeries" among "the most horrific things in life... definitely including abortion" — via Newsweek/GLAAD record (https://www.newsweek.com/tucker-carlson-mocking-transgender-abortion-advocates-says-men-have-babies-too-1625680)
Repeated on-air treatment of youth gender medicine as mutilation; emphatic support for state bans.

### s10 · score −2
Ballot measures mean "encourag[ing] their kids to do drugs, who's benefiting here?" — Nov 2023, on Ohio legalization (https://www.wbur.org/hereandnow/2023/04/24/us-polarizing-issues-abortion; quote via search record of his 2023 commentary, also https://en.wikiquote.org/wiki/Tucker_Carlson)
Repeatedly casts marijuana legalization as elite-sponsored degradation.

### s11 · score +2
Years of nightly segments attacking DEI and race-conscious hiring as anti-white discrimination, catalogued in his Fox record (https://www.justsecurity.org/77299/the-tucker-carlson-disinformation-show/). Emphatic, repeated agreement with strict race/gender-blindness.

### s12 · score +1
"I'm uncomfortable with the death penalty in any circumstance." — retraction to Hannity, 2010, record at Wikipedia (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tucker_Carlson)
Also 2003: opposed "as I am adamantly opposed to abortion." Stated support for abolition, but older and occasionally contradicted (Vick outburst), so +1.

## System
### y01 · score −2
Calls legacy outlets professional liars; e.g. accused media of collaborating with intelligence agencies to deceive the public — record at Just Security (https://www.justsecurity.org/77299/the-tucker-carlson-disinformation-show/). Core, constant theme of his show and network.

### y02 · score −1
Privately texted "Sidney Powell is lying" about machine fraud — Dominion filings, via NBC (https://www.nbcnews.com/politics/donald-trump/private-fox-news-text-messages-emails-released-dominion-suit-rcna72693)
Publicly he has repeatedly called 2020 unfair (rigged by tech/media censorship) while privately rejecting the fraud claims — net hedged disagreement with "administered fairly."

### y03 · score −2
"The Intel committees do not oversee the Intel agencies. They're controlled by the Intel agencies." — 2025 interview circuit (https://patriotfetch.com/2025/09/tucker-carlson-deep-state-media-bias-glen-beck/)
Claims the CIA is preparing a criminal referral against him personally (https://www.wionews.com/photos/-meetings-in-iran-why-tucker-carlson-claims-cia-is-investigating-him-and-why-washington-is-denying-it-1773830949066); treats federal law enforcement as politically weaponized.

### y04 · score +1
Hosted Charlie Kirk on how long Trump has to "smash" the "deep state" before the clock runs out — Daily Caller, Jul 2025 (https://dailycaller.com/2025/07/21/charlie-kirk-tucker-carlson-intelligence-community-deep-state/)
Sympathetic to purging the administrative state, but his 2026 anti-Trump turn makes him warier of raw presidential power; hedged.

### y05 · score 0
Genuinely mixed: condemned BLM-era street protest as mob rule, yet recast Jan 6 defendants as patriots and political prisoners ("Patriot Purge"). Neither consistent support nor opposition to the tactic as such.

### y06 · score +2
Built his post-Fox brand on the "censorship-industrial complex": government pressure on platforms is his archetypal abuse, from Twitter Files coverage to his X move itself (record: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tucker_Carlson). Repeated and emphatic.

### y07 · score 0
Called Roe "the most embarrassing court decision handed down in the last century" (https://en.wikiquote.org/wiki/Tucker_Carlson) but shows no sustained doctrine of defying or delegitimizing the Court itself; mixed record.

### y08 · score +2
Calls the administration "enslaved" by foreign and donor interests and says "There is no future of the MAGA movement." — 2026 Iran-war break (https://www.thedailybeast.com/maga-exile-tucker-carlson-flames-weak-trump-over-iran-surrender/)
The donor-owned uniparty is a constant Carlson theme, now aimed at both parties including Trump.

### y09 · score −2
Climate experts' "bullying is not about helping the Earth, it is about controlling us." — Fox News opinion (https://www.foxnews.com/opinion/tucker-carlson-climate-change-experts-bullying-helping-earth-controlling-us)

### y10 · score +2
"There's a lot of power vested in federal agencies. Congress provides no oversight at all." — 2025 (https://patriotfetch.com/2025/09/tucker-carlson-deep-state-media-bias-glen-beck/)

### y11 · score +1
