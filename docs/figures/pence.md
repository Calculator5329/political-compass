# Mike Pence — evidence dossier
Updated: 2026-07-18 · Researcher: verify-2

Pence in 2026 is the standard-bearer of pre-Trump conservatism: through
Advancing American Freedom and his book "What Conservatives Want" he attacks
Trump's tariffs as "the largest peacetime tax hike," defends free trade, free
enterprise, and entitlement reform, and holds maximal social-conservative
positions (life, faith, youth-gender-medicine bans). On the y-axis he is the
GOP's leading institutionalist — certified the 2020 election, condemned the
Jan 6 pardons and DOJ purges, and insists on due process "for all persons."
