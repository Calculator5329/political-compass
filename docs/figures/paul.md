# Rand Paul — evidence dossier
Updated: 2026-07-18 · Researcher: verify-7

Paul remains the Senate's most consistent libertarian through 2025–26: he co-led
repeated Senate resolutions (April and October 2025) to revoke Trump's Canada
tariffs, voted against the "big, beautiful bill" over deficits, sponsors the
Life at Conception Act and the anti-jawboning Free Speech Protection Act, and
prosecutes a years-long crusade against Fauci, the FBI, and expert consensus.
Right-libertarian on x; moderately insurgent on y — deep institutional distrust
without Trump-style election denial or purge advocacy.
