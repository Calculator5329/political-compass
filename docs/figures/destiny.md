# Destiny (Steven Bonnell II) — evidence dossier
Updated: 2026-07-18 · Researcher: verify-8

Self-described "omniliberal" (X handle @TheOmniLiberal) debate streamer:
pro-capitalism, free-trade, incrementalist social democrat who spent a decade
defending elections, mainstream media, experts, and federal law enforcement
against populists of both flanks — the strongest institutionalist profile in
the online-left space. 2025 complicates the y-axis: after the Kirk
assassination he refused condemnation, endorsed tit-for-tat escalation
("if we do it, hopefully we don't fail like they did"), and now argues Trump
has weaponized the DOJ — his institutionalism has become explicitly
conditional on the other side honoring it.
