# Tulsi Gabbard — evidence dossier
Updated: 2026-07-18 · Researcher: new-3

Gabbard's 2026 profile is defined by her tenure as Trump's Director of National Intelligence (Feb 2025 – mid-2026, stepping down for family reasons): mass declassifications alleging an Obama-era "treasonous conspiracy," a war on the "deep state" and "propaganda media," and firings/clearance revocations inside the IC. Her economics remain her mostly-unrepudiated 2013–2021 progressive congressional record (Medicare for All cosponsor, anti-TCJA vote, marijuana legalization bills), which keeps her near center-left on x, while her system-axis record is emphatically insurgent. Several flips (guns, FISA 702) are scored on current positions per METHOD.
