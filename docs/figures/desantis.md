# Ron DeSantis — evidence dossier
Updated: 2026-07-18 · Researcher: new-1

DeSantis in 2026 is a term-limited Florida governor running the hardest-right
executive record in the states: mass-deportation partnership with ICE, the
Stop WOKE/anti-DEI regime, a six-week abortion ban, permitless carry, and a
drive to abolish homestead property taxes. On the y-axis he mixes real
insurgent signals (deep-state rhetoric, "slitting throats" of the
bureaucracy, media delegitimization, COVID-expert defiance) with a governor's
procedural institutionalism (he certifies elections and never joined 2020
denialism outright).
