# Elon Musk — evidence dossier
Updated: 2026-07-18 · Researcher: verify-6

Musk's 2025–26 arc: DOGE architect of mass federal firings (Jan–May 2025), a
bitter public split with Trump over the "Big Beautiful Bill," founding the
America Party in July 2025 as an anti-deficit "uniparty" protest — then
shelving it by late 2025 to resume mega-funding Republicans for the 2026
midterms ("America is toast if the radical left wins"), with a thawed but
transactional Trump relationship by mid-2026. Economically right-libertarian
but emphatically anti-tariff/free-trade; culturally right (anti-DEI,
anti-"woke," pro-gun, two-sex policy) with pro-legal-immigration and
early-abortion carve-outs; strongly insurgent via deep-state claims, media
and expert contempt, and acted-on demolition of the civil service.
Verification zeroed four econ answers that lacked direct evidence and
hardened death-penalty and religion scores.
