# Donald Trump — evidence dossier
Updated: 2026-07-18 · Researcher: verify-1

Scored on the second-term record: sweeping 2025 tariffs, anti-DEI / two-sexes /
mass-deportation executive orders, Schedule F revival, crypto championing, and
sustained claims that elections, media, and the DOJ/FBI are rigged against him.
Verification confirmed the economic and social rows but corrected three system
scores: the J6 blanket pardon contradicts a −2 on civil disobedience, the
Carr-FCC pressure campaign contradicts a +1 on government jawboning, and the
2025 third-term "methods" talk plus the 2022 "termination" post move y11 to +1.
