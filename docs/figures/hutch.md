Updated: 2026-07-18 · Researcher: verify-9

political streamer. Documented profile: Bernie Sanders supporter who is
"ultimately pragmatic and is happy with incremental progressive policy gains,"
praises the Biden administration, canvassed for Democrats in Wisconsin in 2024,
and self-describes as a pure anti-GOP electoralist ("I want republicans to lose
elections so they don't do harm, I have no other politics rn"). His per-issue
record is thin — almost no quotable single-issue statements surface in search —
so scores rest on (a) directly cited tweets/videos where they exist and (b) ±1
alignment scores tied to his documented Bernie-progressive + Democratic-partisan
profile, with 0 wherever even that inference is a stretch. The previous scoring
used ±2 across the board on alignment alone; that overstates the evidence.
