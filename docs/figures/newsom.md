# Gavin Newsom — evidence dossier
Updated: 2026-07-18 · Researcher: verify-3

California's governor and a presumptive 2028 contender, Newsom in 2025–26
pairs a solidly progressive record (abortion rights in the constitution, $20
fast-food wage, execution moratorium, climate mandates) with deliberate
moderation (retreat from single-payer, wealth-tax opposition, strikers'-UI
veto, "deeply unfair" on trans athletes). On the y-axis he is an
institutionalist who fights Trump through courts and ballot measures — his
"fight fire with fire" Prop 50 redistricting win bends norms but works
entirely inside legal machinery.

## Per-question evidence

Acted directly against broad tariffs; emphatic disagreement.

### e02 · score +1
