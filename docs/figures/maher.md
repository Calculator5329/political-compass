# Bill Maher — evidence dossier
Updated: 2026-07-18 · Researcher: new-3

Maher in 2026 is an old-school liberal institutionalist with a loud contrarian streak: pro-choice, pro-weed, emphatically anti-religion, pro-climate-action, and a defender of elections, courts-as-umpires, and cross-party dinner-table politics — while hammering "woke" Democrats, trans-youth medicine, student-debt forgiveness, tax-the-rich rhetoric, and the medical establishment. He calls Trump's second term a "slow-moving coup" yet insists the system's checks are holding. Placement: center-left with strong institutionalist (−y) pull and several econ/social defections rightward.
