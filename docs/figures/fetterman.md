# John Fetterman — evidence dossier
Updated: 2026-07-18 · Researcher: new-1

Fetterman in 2026 is the Democrats' most Trump-aligned senator (26% support
score in 2025, highest of any Senate Democrat): pro-tariff ("the trade war is
going well"), pro-ICE on criminal deportations, sole Democratic vote for AG
Bondi, GENIUS Act yes, hardline on Israel to the point of threatening to
leave the party. Yet he keeps a working-class-left economic core — unions,
$15 minimum wage, Medicaid, weed legalization — and a loud institutionalist
streak: condemning protest "anarchy" and (mostly) defending the filibuster.
