# Liz Cheney — evidence dossier
Updated: 2026-07-18 · Researcher: verify-7

Cheney remains an orthodox free-trade, low-tax, hawkish conservative on
economics and most social policy — she called Trump's tariffs "fundamentally
an anti-conservative policy" — while her defining post-2020 identity is
institutionalist: J6 committee vice-chair, defender of elections, courts, and
career civil servants, and through 2025–26 an active campaigner for
"guardrails of democracy" and a 2026 anti-Trump congressional majority.
Right on x, strongly institutionalist (negative y). Verification moved two
thin social answers (legal-immigration expansion, youth-gender-medicine bans)
to 0 for lack of concrete supporting evidence.
