# Kamala Harris — evidence dossier
Updated: 2026-07-18 · Researcher: verify-3

Scored on her 2024 platform and 2025–26 post-campaign positioning (107 Days
memoir and 2026 book tour; declined the California governor's race, left the
2028 door open). Economically center-left with deliberate 2024 moderation
(dropped Medicare for All, pro-fracking, crypto-friendly), socially
progressive across the board, and institutionalist on the y-axis — she
presided over the certification of her own defeat — though in 2025–26 she
describes Trump's DOJ as an "abuse of power."
