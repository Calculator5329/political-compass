# Candace Owens — evidence dossier
Updated: 2026-07-18 · Researcher: new-3

By 2026 Owens is a hard-right cultural traditionalist (Catholic convert, anti-abortion, anti-feminist, anti-trans) who has fully exited the MAGA coalition — after the 2026 Iran strikes she called Trump a "genocidal maniac" and urged the 25th Amendment — while keeping a maximally conspiratorial view of media, elections, science, and intelligence agencies. Her economic record is thinner than her culture-war record: reflexively anti-socialist and anti-welfare but not programmatic. Placement: far right on the social axis, moderately right on economics, extreme insurgent on the system axis.
