# Cornel West — evidence dossier
Updated: 2026-07-18 · Researcher: verify-6

West's 2024 independent run codified a democratic-socialist platform (Medicare
for All, wealth taxes, abolishing poverty, canceling student debt, abolishing
ICE, ending new drilling) wrapped in his lifelong prophetic-Christian critique
of the "corporate duopoly," corporate media, and the carceral state; into
2025–26 he organizes against Trump's "neofascist" turn while insisting both
parties are "beyond redemption." Far left on x; strongly insurgent on y via
civil disobedience, duopoly critique, and calls to remake the system.
Verification hardened his gun-control score (platform pillar → −2) and zeroed
two answers (crypto, deep-state) that had no findable direct evidence.

## Per-question evidence
### e01 · score 0
West's economic pillars attack corporate trade deals and "Wall Street greed" but nowhere endorse tariff protectionism, and he condemned Trumponomics as war on workers — Ballotpedia platform summary (https://ballotpedia.org/Cornel_West)
Anti-corporate-globalization but no pro-tariff advocacy; genuinely mixed on the statement as written.

### e02 · score +2
Platform "guaranteed Medicare for All as a human right," including dental, vision, mental and long-term care — 2024 campaign pillars via Ballotpedia (https://ballotpedia.org/Cornel_West)

### e03 · score +2

Explicit wealth-tax and higher-capital-gains advocacy as a campaign plank.

### e05 · score +2
