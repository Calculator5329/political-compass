# Stephen Miller — evidence dossier
Updated: 2026-07-18 · Researcher: verify-1

Scored as White House deputy chief of staff for policy — architect of the
mass-deportation drive (3,000-arrests/day demand, habeas-suspension musing),
the anti-DEI / two-sexes / merit executive orders, and the loudest voice
branding courts, media, and the civil service as enemies of the elected
executive. His social and system rows are among the best-documented in the set.
Verification pulled three economic answers back to 0: unlike Trump and Vance,
Miller has no findable personal record on tech breakup, paid leave/child care,
or crypto — the old non-zero scores imputed administration positions to him
without evidence.
