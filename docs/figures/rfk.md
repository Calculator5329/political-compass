# Robert F. Kennedy Jr. — evidence dossier
Updated: 2026-07-18 · Researcher: verify-7

As HHS Secretary through 2025–26, Kennedy converted a heterodox-populist
campaign platform ($15 minimum wage, Bitcoin evangelism, anti-corporate-capture)
into hard anti-institutional action: firing the entire ACIP vaccine panel,
laying off a quarter-plus of CDC staff, a censorship crusade, two-sex HHS
guidance, and CMS rules barring pediatric gender medicine. Economically
left-leaning-heterodox on x; strongly insurgent on y. Verification moved two
social answers: abortion to genuinely-mixed (his HHS actions now cut against
his stated pro-choice position) and the two-sex question to +2 (acted on).
