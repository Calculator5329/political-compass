# David Pakman — evidence dossier
Updated: 2026-07-18 · Researcher: verify-8

Progressive social-democrat commentator (The David Pakman Show, ~5M+ subs) whose
2025 book *The Echo Machine* frames right-wing media as a post-truth machine and
explicitly argues for **incrementalism over accelerationism**. Left on economics
(single-payer, unions, debt relief, climate), left on social issues, and an
institutional defender on elections/experts — though by 2026 he treats Trump's
DOJ as weaponized and the Roberts Court as politicized, pulling him slightly off
the pure-institutionalist floor.
