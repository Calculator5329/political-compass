# Tim Pool — evidence dossier
Updated: 2026-07-18 · Researcher: verify-9

Occupy-era street journalist turned MAGA-aligned podcaster (Timcast IRL). By
2024-26 he is emphatically pro-tariff, restrictionist on legal and illegal
immigration, anti-CRT/DEI, pro-2A, two-sexes on gender, and radically
system-distrusting: media, 2020 election, FBI/DOJ, experts, and an explicit
"there is a deep state." Seated in the White House new-media press seat from
March 2025, but turned sharply on Trump over Epstein and economy gaslighting in
late 2025-early 2026 — his insurgency outranks his partisanship. Residual-left
holdovers (public healthcare in principle, drug legalization, 2019 support for
a student-loan fix) survive mostly as rhetoric.
