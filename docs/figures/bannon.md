# Steve Bannon — evidence dossier
Updated: 2026-07-18 · Researcher: verify-2

Bannon in 2026 is the loudest ideologue of MAGA economic nationalism: he wants
higher taxes on the wealthy and corporations, tariffs as permanent doctrine, and
war on the "broligarchs" — positions well to the left of GOP orthodoxy on
economics — fused with maximal social traditionalism, mass-deportation
absolutism (including legal H-1B holders), election denial, third-term advocacy,
and open hostility to courts, media, experts, and the administrative state. His
x is pulled left by economics and hard right by social positions; his y is near
the insurgent ceiling.

## Per-question evidence

Tariffs are core War Room doctrine; he touts them as restoring working-class jobs and raising ~$300B revenue (https://warroom.org/sitrep-economic-populism-reloaded-joe-lavorgna-breaks-down-trumps-trade-growth-gameplan/).
