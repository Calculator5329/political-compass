# Elizabeth Warren — evidence dossier
Updated: 2026-07-18 · Researcher: new-1

Warren in 2026 is the Senate's leading economic progressive: Banking
Committee ranking member fighting the GENIUS Act and crypto market-structure
bills, re-filing her Ultra-Millionaire wealth tax, and anchoring the Democratic
counterattack on Trump's tariffs, DOGE firings, and FCC media pressure. She is
far-left on the x-axis but structurally reformist rather than insurgent on the
y-axis — she wants to expand the Court and break donor power, yet defends
elections, the press, and expert-driven policymaking.
