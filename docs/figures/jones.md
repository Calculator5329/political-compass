# Alex Jones — evidence dossier
Updated: 2026-07-18 · Researcher: verify-2

Jones in 2026 remains the archetypal insurgent: every institution — media,
elections, courts, agencies, experts — is an arm of a "globalist" conspiracy,
and even the Trump cabinet contains "deep state elements." His x is hard right
on social questions (abortion as satanic, anti-trans absolutism, gun
maximalism, mass-deportation cheerleading) with paleolibertarian economics
(anti-tax, anti-CBDC/pro-Bitcoin, climate-hoax) tempered only by his demand to
break up Big Tech. His y sits at the insurgent ceiling.
