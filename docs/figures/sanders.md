# Bernie Sanders — evidence dossier
Updated: 2026-07-18 · Researcher: verify-4

Sanders in 2026 is the anchor of the economic left — Medicare for All,
wealth tax, $17 minimum wage, break-up-big-tech — running the Fighting
Oligarchy tour against "government of the billionaire class." His system
posture is genuinely hybrid: fierce anti-establishment critique of donors,
corporate media, and a "corrupt" Supreme Court, combined with emphatic
defense of elections, the civil service, and the rule of law against Trump's
authoritarianism, plus a real cross-aisle legislative record.
