# Brian Tyler Cohen — evidence dossier
Updated: 2026-07-18 · Researcher: verify-8

Highest-viewed Democratic partisan messenger on YouTube; author of the #1 NYT
bestseller *Shameless: Republicans' Deliberate Dysfunction and the Battle to
Preserve Democracy* (2024). His content is anti-Trump/pro-Democratic-agenda
message discipline rather than personal policy heterodoxy: left-of-center on
economics and social issues, fiercely institutionalist on elections and the
"deep state" — while building his brand on attacking mainstream-media framing
("sanewashing") and the captured Supreme Court, and personally headlining No
Kings protests in 2025.
