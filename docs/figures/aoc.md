# Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez — evidence dossier
Updated: 2026-07-18 · Researcher: verify-5

As of 2026, AOC is the progressive left's most visible House member: Green New
Deal author, Medicare-for-All cosponsor, and a leading voice against Trump-era
deportations and executive overreach. Her x placement is driven by maximal
social-democratic economics and social liberalism; her y placement is moderated
by a distinctive split — insurgent tactics (protest, impeachment articles
against justices, court expansion) paired with emphatic defense of elections
and civil servants against conspiracist framing.
